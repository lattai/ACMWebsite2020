Regional Programming Contests

Every year our club forms contest teams and participates in regional progamming contests. There are usually two contests that we participate, one in the fall semester and one in the spring semester.

The Mid-Atlantic Regional ACM Programming Contest takes place in November and we usually go to Shippensburg University to participate.

The Dickinson College Programming Contest takes place in April at Dickinson College. The scale of this contest is smaller than the one in the fall, but the rules are similar for both.



Requirements to join

We don't restrict opportunities only to club members or computer science major. However, spots are limited and priority is given to club members and CS majors during consideration.

There isn't a minimum required CS course for attending the contest, but we recommend participants to complete (or currently taking) CS 216 Data Structure to join the team.

If you are interested in joining us, please email: tangyi01@gettysburg.edu.
